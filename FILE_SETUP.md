# File Setup Guide

## 📁 Files You Need to Add

### 1. Your Profile Photo
**Filename:** `sureshkumar-photo.jpg`
- **Location:** Place in the `e:\zz` folder
- **Recommended size:** 500x500px
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Will appear in:** Both hero section and About section

### 2. Your Resume PDF
**Filename:** `resume.pdf`
- **Location:** Place in the `e:\zz` folder
- **Format:** PDF
- **Will appear as:** Download button in hero section

## 🚀 How to Add Your Files

1. **Copy your profile photo** to `e:\zz\sureshkumar-photo.jpg`
2. **Copy your resume PDF** to `e:\zz\resume.pdf`
3. **Refresh browser** - Press F5

## ✅ What Will Work

✅ **Profile photo** - Appears in hero and About sections  
✅ **Resume download** - Button in hero section  
✅ **Professional layout** - Circular photos with borders  
✅ **Download functionality** - Users can download your resume  

## 🎯 Tips

- **Photo quality:** Use a professional headshot
- **Resume format:** Make sure it's a clean, professional PDF
- **File names:** Use exactly these filenames for it to work automatically

## 🔄 If Files Don't Appear

1. **Check filenames** - Must match exactly
2. **Check location** - Files must be in `e:\zz` folder
3. **Refresh browser** - Press Ctrl+F5 for hard refresh

Your portfolio will automatically display your photo and resume once you add these files!
